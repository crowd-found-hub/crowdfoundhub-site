I joined Crowd Found Hub to build a platform I will use to build all my other projects on

I have always been a fixer. After twenty years of working in medicine and surgery I really started to look at problems on a larger scale. I kept running into the same road blocks, like 'why is my patient's data not secret or secure' or 'why are insurance companies making decisions about my patient's care' or 'why is it so hard for patients to follow non-pharmaceutical approaches?'

Discovering the power of decentralization and self empowerment through Bitcoin and SafeNetwork was life changing moment the instant I discovered the whitepapers. I have a strong passion for finding ways we can use computers to ensure social agreements and reduce common human failures with centralized third party power.

I left my big office, high paying corporate job (Sad to find in medicine anyway) and began to focus all of my work energy into promoting technologies that empower the individual.  I plan on using Crowd Found Hub to build a truly decentralized electronic medical record system; a electric car charging network that rewards the communities that build it; an uploading/SAFE-farming service and offline data storage platform; and further perfect the crowd funding algorithm I help create for the Hub.

I have been very fortunate to be given the opportunity to labor in freedom, but I will not be satisfied until anyone who wants to participate can.

SAFEcrypt combines the power of offline data storage with the power of SAFE network's online system. You pay one time and your data is available forever. Using SAFE networks autonomous-authentication and self-encryption and no server network gives you 100% control over your data.
